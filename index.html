Useful Binds

Open console (~) and type:

bind "key" "command"

  • "hg_change_posture 2" - Bind to specific posture
  • "hg_change_posture" - Cycle through postures / see list in console
  • "fake" - Ragdolize your player
  • "say *drop" - Drop current weapon
  • "+hmcd_holdbreath" - Hold breath (Useful for aiming or poison/gas)
  • "hmcd_togglelaser" - Toggle laser attachment (Works on tasers too)
  • "hg_kick2" - Kick
  • "suicide" - Suicide
  • "+alt1" - Lean Left
  • "+alt2" - Lean Right
  • "+altlook" - Free look (Look around without turning body)
